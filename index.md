## Is There an Official Thunderbird Customer Care Number?

One of the biggest misconceptions among users is that Mozilla offers a direct Thunderbird customer care number. Thunderbird does not provide phone-based customer support.

Because it is an open-source, community-driven project, Thunderbird’s support system is different from commercial software. The developers and community volunteers provide help through online platforms rather than a call center.

However, you can still get reliable and fast support through Thunderbird’s official channels, as well as other trusted resources.

## Tips for a Smooth Thunderbird Experience

- Avoid incompatible add-ons

These small steps help reduce errors and improve reliability.
